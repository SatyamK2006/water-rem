The Importance of Drinking Water

Water supports every system in the body. Staying hydrated helps regulate body temperature, lubricate joints, transport nutrients, support brain function, and maintain healthy skin.

As a general guideline, aim for 2–3 liters per day depending on your body, climate, and activity level. Listen to your thirst cues, and increase intake during exercise and hot weather.

Signs and Side Effects of Dehydration

  • Dry mouth, thirst, and dark yellow urine
  • Headaches, dizziness, and fatigue
  • Reduced concentration and mood changes
  • Muscle cramps and reduced physical performance
  • Constipation and kidney stress when prolonged

Simple Hydration Tips

  • Keep a reusable bottle nearby and sip regularly
  • Set periodic reminders (use the app’s reminder feature)
  • Flavor your water with lemon, mint, or cucumber
  • Eat water-rich foods like fruits and vegetables
  • Drink extra water before, during, and after exercise
